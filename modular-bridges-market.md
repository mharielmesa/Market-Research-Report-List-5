<p><p>The modular bridges market includes various types based on design and structure. **Beam Type** bridges use horizontal beams supported by piers, ideal for short to medium spans due to simplicity and cost-effectiveness. **Arch Type** bridges feature curved structures that distribute weight efficiently, suitable for longer spans and aesthetic appeal. **Suspension Type** bridges utilize cables and towers to support the deck, enabling very long spans and flexibility. **Others** include truss, cable-stayed, or movable bridges, catering to specific needs like heavy loads or limited space. Each type offers unique advantages in construction and application.</p></p>
