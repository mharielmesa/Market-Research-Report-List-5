<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automatic Transfer Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open transition</li><li>Closed transition</li><li>Static transfer switch (STS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS) ensure seamless power transition between primary and backup sources. **Open Transition** switches briefly disconnect power during transfer, suitable for non-critical loads. **Closed Transition** switches overlap power sources momentarily, ensuring uninterrupted supply, ideal for sensitive equipment. **Static Transfer Switch (STS)** uses solid-state technology for ultra-fast transfers, often in milliseconds, perfect for high-availability systems. **Others** include specialized ATS types like delayed transition or hybrid systems, catering to unique operational needs. Each type serves distinct applications based on load sensitivity and transfer speed requirements.</p></p>
