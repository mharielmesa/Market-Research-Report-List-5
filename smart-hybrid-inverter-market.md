<p><p>The Smart Hybrid Inverter market comprises two main types: Single-Phase and Three-Phase Inverters. Single-Phase Inverters are commonly used in residential settings, converting direct current (DC) from solar panels into alternating current (AC) for home use, typically serving lower power needs. In contrast, Three-Phase Inverters are designed for commercial and industrial applications, managing higher power loads efficiently and balancing electrical loads across three phases for enhanced performance and reliability. Both types facilitate energy storage integration and grid connectivity.</p></p>
