<p><p>The Industrial Remote Terminal Unit (RTU) market is divided into two primary types: wireless and wired RTUs. Wireless Industrial RTUs utilize radio, cellular, or satellite communication to transmit data, allowing for greater flexibility and easier installation in remote locations. In contrast, Wired Industrial RTUs rely on physical connections, such as Ethernet or serial interfaces, to transfer data, offering reliability and security. Both types cater to various applications, including monitoring, control, and automation in industrial settings.</p></p>
