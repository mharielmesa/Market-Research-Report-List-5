<p><p>Pre-Crash Seatbelt market types encompass advanced safety designs aimed at reducing injury during vehicle collisions. The Preload Type Belt tightens the seatbelt gradually before impact, enhancing occupant restraint. This proactive approach stabilizes positions, minimizing forward motion during a crash. In contrast, the Preroll Preload Type Belt combines this functionality with a rolling mechanism that adjusts seatbelt tension based on pre-crash dynamics, improving adaptability. Together, these technologies support improved safety measures and enhance occupant protection in increasingly sophisticated automotive systems.</p></p>
