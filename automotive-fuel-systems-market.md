<p><p>The automotive fuel systems market encompasses various types, including gasoline systems, diesel oil systems, and CNG or LPG systems. Gasoline systems utilize spark-ignited engines and are widely used due to their efficiency and popularity. Diesel oil systems rely on compression ignition, offering higher torque and fuel economy, making them preferred in heavy-duty vehicles. CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) and LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas) systems are alternative fuels that provide cleaner emissions and are gaining traction in eco-friendly transportation markets, appealing to environmentally-conscious consumers.</p></p>
