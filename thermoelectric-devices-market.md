<p><p>The thermoelectric devices market is categorized into three main types: bulk thermoelectric devices, micro thermoelectric devices, and thin film thermoelectric devices. Bulk thermoelectric devices, typically made from heavy materials, are widely used in power generation and refrigeration. Micro thermoelectric devices are miniaturized systems suitable for portable applications and energy harvesting in small electronic devices. Thin film thermoelectric devices offer enhanced performance due to their reduced thickness, making them ideal for integration into flexible electronics and advanced energy management systems.</p></p>
