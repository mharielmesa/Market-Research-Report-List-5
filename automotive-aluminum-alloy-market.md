<p><p>The automotive aluminum alloy market comprises three main types: cast aluminum alloys, rolled aluminum alloys, and extruded aluminum alloys. Cast aluminum alloys are used for complex shapes and components like engine blocks. Rolled aluminum alloys are processed into sheets and used in body panels and structural parts, offering lightweight and high-strength properties. Extruded aluminum alloys are formed into specific profiles, ideal for components like frames and supports. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing vehicle performance, efficiency, and sustainability.</p></p>
