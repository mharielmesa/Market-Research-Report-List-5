<p><strong>The OBU (On Board Unit) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sngle-chip OBU (Without IC Card Interface)</li><li>Double-chip OBU (With IC Card Interface)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On Board Unit (OBU) market consists of two primary types: single-chip and double-chip units. Single-chip OBUs operate without an integrated circuit (IC) card interface, making them simpler and typically less costly. They are designed for basic functionalities such as toll collection and vehicle tracking. In contrast, double-chip OBUs include an IC card interface, allowing for enhanced capabilities like secure transactions and access control, making them suitable for more complex applications such as public transportation systems.</p></p>
