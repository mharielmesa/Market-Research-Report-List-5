<p><p>The tabletop Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM) market encompasses various types, including Wide-angle SEM (W-SEM), Field Emission Gun SEM (FEG-SEM), and Focused Ion Beam SEM (FIB-SEM). W-SEM offers extensive field-of-view imaging, ideal for large samples. FEG-SEM provides high-resolution imaging with superior electron flux, suitable for detailed material analysis. FIB-SEM combines milling and imaging capabilities, facilitating precise sample preparation and modification. Together, these technologies cater to diverse research and industrial applications, enhancing material characterization and analysis.</p></p>
