<p><ul><li>Four Channels ABS</li><li>Three Channels ABS</li><li>Two Channels ABS</li><li>Single Channel ABS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS) market includes various types categorized by the number of channels. Four Channels ABS operates independently for each wheel, providing optimal control and stability. Three Channels ABS usually includes two front wheels and one rear, catering to light-duty trucks. Two Channels ABS manages front-wheel control and provides a shared system for the rear wheels, while Single Channel ABS is designed for basic vehicles with a single braking circuit. Each type addresses specific performance and safety needs in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
