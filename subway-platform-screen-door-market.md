<p><p>The subway platform screen door market consists of three main types: Full-Closed Type, Semi-Closed Type, and Half-Height Type. Full-Closed Type doors provide complete separation between the platform and tracks, enhancing safety and climate control. Semi-Closed Type offers partial barriers, allowing for airflow while still improving safety. Half-Height Type doors are lower barriers primarily focused on crowd management without obstructing visibility or airflow. Each type caters to different safety, aesthetic, and operational needs in urban transit systems.</p></p>
