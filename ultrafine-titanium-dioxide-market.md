<p><ul><li>Anatase Based</li><li>Rutile Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrafine titanium dioxide market is primarily divided into two types: anatase-based and rutile-based titanium dioxide. Anatase-based titanium dioxide is favored for applications such as plastics and coatings due to its high surface area and UV absorption properties. In contrast, rutile-based titanium dioxide is preferred for applications requiring higher durability and photocatalytic activity, making it ideal for paints, paper, and cosmetics. Both types are essential in various industries, catering to distinct performance and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
