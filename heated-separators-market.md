<p><p>Heated separators are essential in various industrial applications for separating fluids based on their density differences. The market is primarily divided into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical heated separators utilize a tall, narrow design, which promotes effective gravity separation and is often preferred for limited spaces. Both types serve critical roles in oil and gas, petrochemical, and wastewater treatment industries.</p></p>
