<p><ul><li>Pickling/Immersion Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Spray Metal Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Vapor Phase Metal Cleaning Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parts washers market includes three main types: Pickling/Immersion Cleaning Equipment, Spray Metal Cleaning Equipment, and Vapor Phase Metal Cleaning Equipment. Pickling/immersion cleaning uses chemical baths to remove contaminants from metal surfaces, often used in heavy-duty applications. Spray metal cleaning employs high-pressure sprays for effective cleaning of intricate parts. Vapor phase cleaning utilizes solvents that evaporate, creating a vapor that condenses on parts, effectively removing oils and debris without contact. Each type serves distinct industrial cleaning needs.</p></p>
