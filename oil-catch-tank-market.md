<p><p>An oil catch tank, also known as an oil separator or breather tank, is a device used in internal combustion engines to prevent oil vapors from entering the intake system. It captures blow-by gases and oil mist, separating the oil from the air and returning clean air to the intake while storing the oil for later disposal or reuse. This helps improve engine performance, reduce emissions, and extend engine life by preventing oil buildup in the intake system.</p><p>The Oil Catch Tank Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
