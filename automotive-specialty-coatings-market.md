<p><strong>The Automotive Specialty Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive specialty coatings market comprises three main types: solvent-borne, waterborne, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings utilize organic solvents, providing excellent durability and finish but raising environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. Waterborne coatings, with lower VOC levels, are more eco-friendly and are increasingly used for their ease of application and quick drying properties. Powder coatings, applied as a dry powder, offer a thicker finish and superior resistance to chipping and corrosion, making them ideal for various automotive parts.</p></p>
