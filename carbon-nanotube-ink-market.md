<p><p>The carbon nanotube ink market is primarily divided into two types: multiwall carbon nanotube ink and single wall carbon nanotube ink. Multiwall carbon nanotube ink consists of multiple layers of nanotubes, offering enhanced electrical conductivity and mechanical strength, making it suitable for various applications like composite materials and flexible electronics. Single wall carbon nanotube ink features a single layer of nanotubes, providing superior electrical properties and transparency, making it ideal for applications in sensors, displays, and advanced coatings.</p></p>
