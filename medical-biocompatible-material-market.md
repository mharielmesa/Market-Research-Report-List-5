<p><p>The medical biocompatible material market includes various high-performance polymers, notably PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) and PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone). PEEK is known for its excellent mechanical properties, thermal stability, and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for implants and surgical devices. Conversely, PPSU offers high temperature resistance and superior impact strength, along with good sterilization capabilities, making it suitable for a wide range of medical applications such as surgical instruments and housings for medical devices. Together, these materials enhance patient safety and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
