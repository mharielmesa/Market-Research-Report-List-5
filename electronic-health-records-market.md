<p><strong>The Electronic Health Records Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Web Based</li><li>Client Server Based</li><li>Software as Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Health Records (EHR) market can be categorized into three main types: web-based, client-server based, and software as a service (SaaS). </p><p>Web-based EHR systems are accessed through browsers and require an internet connection, offering flexibility and remote accessibility. Client-server based systems are installed on local servers, providing enhanced control and data security but requiring significant IT infrastructure. SaaS combines aspects of both, offering subscription-based access to cloud-hosted EHR solutions, minimizing maintenance for users while facilitating scalability and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
