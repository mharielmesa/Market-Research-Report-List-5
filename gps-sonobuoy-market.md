<p><p>The GPS sonobuoy market consists of three primary types: active sonobuoys, passive sonobuoys, and special-purpose sonobuoys. Active sonobuoys emit sound waves to detect underwater objects, providing real-time tracking and location data. Passive sonobuoys, on the other hand, listen for sounds and vibrations in the water, ideal for surveillance without alerting targets. Special-purpose sonobuoys are tailored for specific missions, such as environmental monitoring or advanced tactical operations, offering versatile capabilities for diverse maritime applications.</p></p>
