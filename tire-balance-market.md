<p><p>The tire balance market is segmented into Clip-On and Adhesive types, each serving distinct needs. Clip-On weights are easily attached to the rim's edge, offering quick adjustments and are commonly used for steel rims. In contrast, Adhesive weights stick to the interior of the rim, providing a cleaner aesthetic and are typically favored for alloy wheels. Both types ensure optimal tire performance by minimizing vibrations and enhancing vehicle stability, catering to different preferences in automotive applications.</p></p>
