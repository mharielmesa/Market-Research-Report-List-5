<p><strong>The Protective Paints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne Coatings</li><li>Water-borne Coatings</li><li>Powder Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protective paints market is categorized into three main types: solvent-borne coatings, water-borne coatings, and powder coatings. Solvent-borne coatings contain organic solvents, providing durability and resistance but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-borne coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. Powder coatings consist of dry powder applied electrostatically and cured with heat, resulting in a tough finish that's ideal for metal surfaces and minimizing waste during application.</p></p>
