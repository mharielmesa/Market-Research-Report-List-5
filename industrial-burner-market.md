<p><p>The industrial burner market is categorized into three main types: oil-based, gas-based, and dual fuel burners. Oil-based burners primarily use liquid fuels, providing high energy density and efficiency, ideal for specific industrial applications. Gas-based burners utilize natural gas or propane, offering cleaner combustion and lower emissions, making them popular for eco-friendly operations. Dual fuel burners combine both oil and gas capabilities, providing flexibility and operational resilience, allowing industries to switch fuels based on cost or availability.</p></p>
