<p><p>Integral waterproofing admixtures for concrete enhance water resistance by modifying the concrete matrix. The crystalline type features compounds that form crystals in the presence of moisture, sealing voids and preventing water infiltration. Pore blocking types work by filling the pores within the concrete with a hydrophobic material, effectively reducing permeability. Other types may include superabsorbent polymers or hydrophobic agents, which offer unique mechanisms for water resistance. These admixtures find applications in various construction projects, promoting durability and longevity.</p></p>
