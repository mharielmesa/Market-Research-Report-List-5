<p><p>The floor insulation market comprises various types, including glass wool, rock wool, foam, and other materials. Glass wool is made from recycled glass fibers, offering excellent thermal insulation and soundproofing. Rock wool, derived from volcanic rock, provides fire resistance and moisture control. Foam insulation, available in rigid panels or sprayed forms, is known for its high insulating value and air sealing properties. Other materials may include cellulose or polyester, catering to diverse applications and environmental preferences in construction and renovations.</p></p>
