<p><ul><li>General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS)</li><li>High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PS resin market primarily includes two types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is known for its clarity and rigidity, making it suitable for applications like packaging and consumer goods. In contrast, HIPS is modified with rubber to enhance impact resistance, making it ideal for durable products such as appliance housings and automotive components. Both types cater to diverse industries, driven by their distinct properties and performance characteristics, meeting varying customer demands.</p></p>
