<p><p>A gastrostomy tube is a medical device inserted through the abdominal wall into the stomach to provide nutrition to individuals who cannot eat by mouth. It is commonly used in patients with swallowing difficulties due to various medical conditions, allowing for direct delivery of food, liquids, and medications.</p><p>The gastrostomy tube market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of chronic diseases such as neurological disorders, cancers, and head and neck injuries that impair swallowing. Advances in tube designs, materials, and insertion techniques have improved patient comfort and safety, further fueling market demand. The rising geriatric population, which often requires nutritional support, also contributes to market expansion.</p><p>Additionally, an increasing awareness of enteral feeding and technological innovations in gastrostomy products are enhancing market prospects. The adoption of home healthcare alternatives is another trend driving demand, as patients increasingly prefer to manage their nutrition at home.
