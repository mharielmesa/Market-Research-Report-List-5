<p><p>The endoscope robot market features three main types: Multi-Hole Mirror Robots, Single-Hole Mirror Robots, and No-Hole Mirror Robots. Multi-Hole Mirror Robots utilize multiple entry points to enhance visibility and facilitate complex surgeries. Single-Hole Mirror Robots are designed for procedures requiring minimal invasiveness, utilizing a single entry to streamline operation while maintaining efficacy. No-Hole Mirror Robots offer non-invasive options, using advanced imaging technologies for diagnostic purposes without any incisions, catering to patients seeking less invasive interventions.</p></p>
