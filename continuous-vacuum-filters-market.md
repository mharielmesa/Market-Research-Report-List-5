<p><p>The continuous vacuum filters market encompasses various types, primarily including disc filters, drum filters, and drum pre-coat filters. Disc filters utilize a series of discs to separate solids from liquids, while drum filters employ a rotating drum covered with a filter cloth. Drum pre-coat filters enhance filtration by applying a layer of filter aid on the drum before filtration begins. Additionally, other filter types, such as belt filters and leaf filters, cater to specific processing requirements, enhancing efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
