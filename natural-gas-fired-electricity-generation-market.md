<p><p>Natural gas fired electricity generation involves several market types: </p><p>1. **Gas Turbines**: These systems burn natural gas to produce electricity, known for quick startup times and efficiency in peaking power. </p><p>2. **Natural Gas Combined Cycle (NGCC)**: This technology combines gas and steam turbines, utilizing waste heat for improved efficiency and lower emissions, making it a preferred choice for base-load power generation. </p><p>3. **Natural Gas Fuel Cells**: These convert natural gas into electricity through electrochemical processes, offering high efficiency and lower environmental impact, especially in distributed generation applications.</p></p>
