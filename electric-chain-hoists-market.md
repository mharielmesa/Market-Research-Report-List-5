<p><p>Electric chain hoists are categorized into two main types based on their power supply: single-phase and three-phase. Single-phase electric chain hoists operate on a single electrical phase, making them suitable for residential and light industrial applications where power availability is limited. In contrast, three-phase electric chain hoists utilize three-phase power, providing higher efficiency and a greater lifting capacity, ideal for heavy-duty industrial tasks. Each type is designed to meet specific operational needs and power requirements.</p></p>
