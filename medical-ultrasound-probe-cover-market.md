<p><p>The medical ultrasound probe cover market comprises two primary types: latex-free probe covers and latex probe covers. Latex-free probe covers are designed to accommodate patients with latex allergies, ensuring safety and comfort during procedures. These covers are typically made from alternative materials like thermoplastics. In contrast, latex probe covers are made from natural rubber latex, offering a cost-effective option but posing a risk for allergic reactions in sensitive individuals. Both types are essential for maintaining hygiene and preventing cross-contamination in ultrasound examinations.</p></p>
