<p><p>The paints and coatings fillers market encompasses various types based on their formulation and application methods. High solids/radiation curing fillers offer greater durability with lower solvent content, making them environmentally friendly. Powder-based fillers provide excellent coverage and are solvent-free. Waterborne fillers are easier to clean and emit fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-borne fillers typically offer robust adhesion and durability but may have higher VOC emissions. Other categories include specialty formulations designed for specific applications or environments, enhancing performance and versatility.</p></p>
