<p><strong>The Pilot Headsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNR headsets</li><li>ANR headsets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pilot headset market primarily consists of two types: Passive Noise Reduction (PNR) headsets and Active Noise Reduction (ANR) headsets. PNR headsets rely on physical barriers, such as padding and design, to block external noise, making them simpler and often more affordable. In contrast, ANR headsets utilize electronic systems to actively cancel out ambient sounds, providing enhanced comfort and clarity in noisy environments. The choice between PNR and ANR largely depends on personal preference and specific flying conditions.</p></p>
