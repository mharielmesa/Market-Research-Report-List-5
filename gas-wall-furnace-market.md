<p><ul><li>Noncondensing Type</li><li>Condensing Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas wall furnace market comprises two main types: noncondensing and condensing units. Noncondensing furnaces operate by venting exhaust gases directly outside, making them generally less efficient but simpler and less expensive. In contrast, condensing types utilize heat exchangers to extract additional heat from exhaust gases, enhancing efficiency and reducing energy costs. While noncondensing models are suitable for basic heating needs, condensing units are favored for their energy efficiency and lower environmental impact, catering to a more eco-conscious market.</p></p>
