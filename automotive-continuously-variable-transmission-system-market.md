<p><ul><li>Belt-Chain Drive CVT</li><li>Planetary CVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive continuously variable transmission (CVT) market primarily features two types: belt-chain drive CVT and planetary CVT. Belt-chain drive CVT utilizes a belt and pulley system to provide an infinite range of gear ratios, enhancing efficiency and driving comfort. Conversely, planetary CVT employs a set of gears that allow for smooth transitions between different ratios, offering robust performance and compact design. Both types cater to diverse vehicle applications, optimizing fuel efficiency and driving dynamics in modern automotive engineering.</p></p>
