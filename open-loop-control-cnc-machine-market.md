<p><p>An open-loop control CNC machine is a type of computer numerically controlled system that operates without feedback mechanisms to correct performance errors. These machines interpret input commands to control motion and position but do not adjust based on the actual outcomes of those commands. This makes them simpler and generally less expensive than their closed-loop counterparts, although they may lack precision for complex tasks.</p><p>The Open-loop Control CNC Machine Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing automation across industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.
