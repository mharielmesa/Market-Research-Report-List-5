<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Base Coat</li><li>Clearcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic coating in the automotive market involves three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. The primer serves as the foundation, ensuring proper adhesion and enhancing corrosion resistance. The base coat provides color and aesthetics, significantly impacting the vehicle's visual appeal. Lastly, the clearcoat offers a protective layer that shields the underlying paint from UV rays, scratches, and chemical exposure, ensuring durability and long-lasting finish. Together, these coatings enhance both the functionality and appearance of automotive surfaces.</p></p>
