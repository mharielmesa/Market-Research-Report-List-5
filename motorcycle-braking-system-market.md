<p><p>The motorcycle braking system market is primarily divided into two main types: disc brakes and drum brakes. Disc brakes are known for their superior stopping power, heat dissipation, and responsiveness, making them popular in modern motorcycles. They consist of a rotor and caliper system. Conversely, drum brakes feature a enclosed design with brake shoes that expand against a drum. They are generally more cost-effective and are commonly found in older models and entry-level motorcycles. Both systems have unique advantages based on performance and usage.</p></p>
