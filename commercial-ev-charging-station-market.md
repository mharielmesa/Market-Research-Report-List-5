Chaoji is a fast-charging standard developed in China, offering high power and compatibility. CCS Type I, common in North America, combines AC and DC charging in a single connector. CCS Type II, widely used in Europe, supports higher power levels and bidirectional charging. These systems cater to regional standards, ensuring efficient and scalable EV charging infrastructure globally.</p></p>
