<p><ul><li>Analog Output Hall Sensor</li><li>Digital Output Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hall Effect Sensor IC market is primarily segmented into two types: Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage proportional to the magnetic field strength, allowing for precise measurements in applications like motor control and position sensing. In contrast, Digital Output Hall Sensors convert magnetic field changes into discrete signals, offering on/off detection suitable for applications like proximity sensing and speed detection. Both types serve crucial roles in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
