<p><ul><li>Volumetric Pressure Booster</li><li>Centrifugal Pressure Booster</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure booster market includes several types, primarily categorized into volumetric and centrifugal pressure boosters. Volumetric pressure boosters operate by trapping a specific volume of water and displacing it to achieve higher pressures, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent flow. Centrifugal pressure boosters, on the other hand, use a rotating impeller to increase water velocity and pressure, suitable for various industrial applications. Additionally, there are other specialized pressure boosters that cater to niche requirements within specific industries.</p></p>
