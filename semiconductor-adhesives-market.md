<p><ul><li>Liquid</li><li>Solid</li><li>Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor adhesives market comprises three main types: liquid, solid, and paste adhesives. Liquid adhesives offer excellent flow properties and can fill gaps in intricate designs, making them ideal for intricate semiconductor bonding. Solid adhesives provide robust mechanical strength and stability, suitable for permanent bonds in harsh environments. Paste adhesives, often used in printed circuit board assembly, allow for precise application and good adhesion while ensuring thermal and electrical conductivity. Each type caters to specific application needs in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
