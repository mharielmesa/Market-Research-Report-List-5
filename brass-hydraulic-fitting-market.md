<p><p>The Brass Hydraulic Fitting market consists of various types, including Sleeve Type Pipe Joints and Welded Pipe Joints. Sleeve Type Pipe Joints provide ease of installation and disassembly, utilizing a sleeve that encases the pipe ends for a secure connection without welding. In contrast, Welded Pipe Joints involve permanently joining pipes through welding, ensuring a stronger, leak-proof bond suitable for high-pressure applications. Both types are essential for reliable hydraulic systems, offering unique benefits based on specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
