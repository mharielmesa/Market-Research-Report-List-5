<p><strong>The Automotive Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Way Catalyst</li><li>Three Way Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Catalyst Market includes Two-Way and Three-Way Catalysts, both designed to reduce vehicle emissions. Two-Way Catalysts primarily oxidize carbon monoxide (CO) and unburned hydrocarbons (HC) into less harmful substances like carbon dioxide (CO₂) and water (H₂O). They are commonly used in diesel engines. Three-Way Catalysts, used in gasoline engines, additionally reduce nitrogen oxides (NOx) into nitrogen (N₂) and oxygen (O₂), alongside oxidizing CO and HC. Three-Way Catalysts are more advanced, addressing a broader range of pollutants for stricter emission standards.</p></p>
