<p><strong>The Leather Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne</li><li>Waterborne</li><li>UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leather coatings market is categorized into solventborne, waterborne, and UV-cured coatings. Solventborne coatings utilize organic solvents, offering robust adhesion and durability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Waterborne coatings use water as a primary solvent, providing an eco-friendlier option with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs), appealing to environmentally conscious consumers. UV-cured coatings rely on ultraviolet light for rapid curing, resulting in strong, durable finishes. Each type serves specific requirements in quality, environmental impact, and performance.</p></p>
