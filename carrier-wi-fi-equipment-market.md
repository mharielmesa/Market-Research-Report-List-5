<p><p>The Carrier Wi-Fi Equipment market encompasses two primary types: Wireless Access Points (WAPs) and Wireless LAN Controllers (WLCs). Wireless Access Points are devices that facilitate wireless connectivity for users, enabling multiple devices to connect to a network and providing extended coverage. Wireless LAN Controllers manage and orchestrate multiple WAPs, ensuring seamless connectivity, security, and centralized management. Together, these components enhance network performance, scalability, and efficiency in delivering high-speed internet access, particularly in dense urban environments and large enterprises.</p></p>
