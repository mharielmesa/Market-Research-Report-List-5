<p><p>The ion-exchange resin market is segmented into cation exchange resins and anion exchange resins. Cation exchange resins are designed to exchange positive ions, effectively removing harmful cations like heavy metals from water, making them essential in water treatment and softening applications. Anion exchange resins, on the other hand, target negatively charged ions, assisting in the removal of contaminants such as nitrates and sulfates. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food processing, and environmental remediation.</p></p>
