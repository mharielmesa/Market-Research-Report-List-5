<p><p>The Titanium Dioxide market primarily consists of two production types: Sulfuric Acid Titanium Dioxide and Chlorination Titanium Dioxide. Sulfuric Acid Titanium Dioxide involves the use of sulfuric acid in a sulfate process to produce a cost-effective pigment suitable for coatings and plastics. In contrast, Chlorination Titanium Dioxide utilizes titanium tetrachloride, producing a higher quality, more refined product, often used in high-performance applications like specialized coatings and advanced materials. Both methods cater to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
