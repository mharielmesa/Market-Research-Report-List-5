<p><p>The Full Ice Protection System (FIPS) market is crucial for ensuring aircraft safety by preventing ice accumulation. It comprises two main types: De-Icing Systems and Anti-Icing Systems. De-Icing Systems actively remove ice that has already formed on surfaces during flight, while Anti-Icing Systems prevent ice from forming in the first place. Both systems are essential for maintaining aircraft performance and safety in varying weather conditions, catering to the growing demand for reliable ice protection solutions in aviation.</p></p>
