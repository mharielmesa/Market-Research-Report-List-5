<p><p>A grating ruler is a precision measurement tool used in various industries, including manufacturing, electronics, and metrology, to ensure high accuracy in linear displacement measurements. Grating rulers are essential in applications requiring micron-level accuracy, such as CNC machining, semiconductor manufacturing, and quality control processes.</p><p>The Grating Ruler Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for high-precision measurement tools in advanced manufacturing and automation industries.
