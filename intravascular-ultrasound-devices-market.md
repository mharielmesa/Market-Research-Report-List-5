<p><p>The intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) devices market comprises three main types: IVUS consoles, IVUS catheters, and IVUS accessories. IVUS consoles provide the imaging and processing capabilities, enabling visualization of vascular structures. IVUS catheters are specialized tools that are inserted into blood vessels to capture high-resolution images of the arterial walls. IVUS accessories include various supplementary products necessary for the operation and maintenance of IVUS systems, enhancing their functionality and ensuring effective diagnostic procedures in cardiology and vascular interventions.</p></p>
