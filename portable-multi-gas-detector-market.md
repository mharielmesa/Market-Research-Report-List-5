<p><p>The Portable Multi Gas Detector market includes various technologies for detecting hazardous gases. Catalytic combustion sensors measure flammable gases based on their combustion heat. Electrochemical sensors (echem) use chemical reactions to identify specific gases, making them ideal for toxic gas detection. Infrared (IR) detectors utilize light absorption properties to identify gas concentrations, particularly for hydrocarbons and CO2. Photoionization detectors (PID) employ UV light to ionize gases, enabling the detection of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other low-concentration gases.</p></p>
