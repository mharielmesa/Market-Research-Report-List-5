They are commonly used in applications requiring frequent assembly and disassembly. In contrast, Welding Type joints are directly welded to the piping, ensuring a more rigid connection ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types play crucial roles in managing thermal expansion and ensuring structural integrity in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
