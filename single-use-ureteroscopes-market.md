<p><p>The single-use ureteroscopes market consists of two main types: digital ureteroscopes and fiberoptic ureteroscopes. Digital ureteroscopes utilize advanced imaging technology, offering enhanced visualization and diagnostic capabilities, making them suitable for complex procedures. In contrast, fiberoptic ureteroscopes employ traditional fiber optics to transmit images, providing reliable performance but with limited imaging enhancements. The choice between these types depends on factors such as procedural needs, costs, and the desire for reduced risk of cross-contamination, with single-use options growing in popularity for infection control.</p></p>
