<p><p>Corrosion and scale inhibitors are chemical compounds used to prevent or reduce the degradation of materials caused by corrosion and the formation of mineral scales in industrial systems. Corrosion inhibitors work by forming a protective layer on metal surfaces, preventing chemical reactions that lead to rust or material breakdown. Scale inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent the accumulation of mineral deposits, such as calcium carbonate or sulfate, which can clog pipelines and reduce system efficiency. These inhibitors are widely used in industries like oil and gas, power generation, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p><p>The Corrosion and Scale Inhibitors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period.
