<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Disinfection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection</li><li>High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection</li><li>Ozone UV Disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultraviolet disinfection market includes several key types. Ordinary cathode low-pressure mercury UV disinfection systems use low-pressure mercury lamps to emit UV light, effectively killing pathogens in water and air. High-intensity ultraviolet disinfection utilizes medium- to high-pressure mercury lamps for enhanced efficacy, addressing larger volumes and more challenging contaminants. Ozone UV disinfection combines ultraviolet light and ozone to improve disinfection efficiency, often employed in water treatment processes for enhanced microbial inactivation and oxidation of organic pollutants.</p></p>
