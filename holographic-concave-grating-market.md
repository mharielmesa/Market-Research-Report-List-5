<p><p>The holographic concave grating market comprises two primary types: transmission gratings and reflective gratings. Transmission gratings allow light to pass through, using holography to achieve high resolution and efficiency for wavelength dispersion in optical systems. Reflective gratings, on the other hand, function by reflecting light off their surface, typically used in applications requiring compact designs and high sensitivity. Both types are essential in spectroscopy, telecommunications, and other optical applications, offering distinct advantages depending on the user's needs.</p></p>
