<p><p>The CRT Defibrillator (CRT-D) market is categorized into two main types: internal and external defibrillators. Internal defibrillators, typically implanted in patients with severe heart rhythm disorders, provide continuous monitoring and automated shocks to restore normal heart function. External defibrillators, used primarily in emergency settings, are portable devices that deliver shocks to patients in cardiac arrest. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes by restoring effective heart rhythm, addressing different clinical needs and settings in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
