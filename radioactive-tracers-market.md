<p><strong>The Radioactive Tracers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PET</li><li>SPECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radioactive tracers market encompasses two primary types: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). PET utilizes positron-emitting isotopes to provide detailed images of metabolic processes, making it invaluable in oncology and cardiology. SPECT employs gamma-emitting isotopes, offering functional imaging for various diseases, especially in neurology and cardiology. Both modalities rely on radioactive tracers to visualize biological processes, playing crucial roles in diagnostics, treatment planning, and monitoring patient responses in clinical settings.</p></p>
