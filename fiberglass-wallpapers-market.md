<p><p>Fiberglass wallpapers are categorized into non-woven and woven fabric types, each offering distinct properties. Non-woven fiberglass wallpapers are made from a blend of cellulose and synthetic fibers, making them lightweight, breathable, and easy to install and remove. They are resistant to moisture and mold, enhancing durability. In contrast, woven fiberglass wallpapers feature an interlaced construction, providing superior strength and texture. They are ideal for high-traffic areas due to their robustness, offering aesthetic appeal along with long-lasting performance.</p></p>
