<p><strong>The Protein Production Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prokaryotic Expression Systems</li><li>Mammalian Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Yeast Cell Expression Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protein production market encompasses various expression systems tailored for different applications. Prokaryotic expression systems, such as bacterial cultures, offer rapid and cost-effective protein synthesis but may lack post-translational modifications. Mammalian cell expression systems are preferred for producing complex proteins with correct modifications, despite being more time-consuming and expensive. Yeast cell expression systems provide a middle ground, combining advantages of both prokaryotic and mammalian cells. Other systems include insect cells or plant-based methods, each serving specific niche requirements in protein production.</p></p>
