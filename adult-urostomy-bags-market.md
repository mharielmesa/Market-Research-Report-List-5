<p><p>The adult urostomy bags market is primarily categorized into two types based on flange design: flat flanges and convex flanges. Flat flanges provide a level surface for skin contact, making them suitable for patients with even abdominal contours. In contrast, convex flanges are designed with a heightened border, ideal for individuals with uneven or recessed skin areas, allowing for better adherence and less risk of leakage. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing comfort and security in urostomy care.</p></p>
