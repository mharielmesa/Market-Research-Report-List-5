<p><p>The multi-mode microplate readers market consists of three primary types: filter-based readers, monochromator-based readers, and hybrid readers. Filter-based readers use specific filters to isolate wavelengths for various assays, offering simplicity and reliability. Monochromator-based readers provide greater flexibility by allowing users to choose any wavelength within a range, enhancing versatility for diverse applications. Hybrid readers combine both technologies, enabling improved adaptability and broader functionality for complex analyses, catering to diverse research needs in laboratories and clinical settings.</p></p>
