<p><p>The immunology market is categorized into immuno boosters and immunosuppressants. Immuno boosters enhance the immune system's response, helping the body fight infections and diseases, often used in preventive healthcare and aging populations. In contrast, immunosuppressants are designed to inhibit immune responses, crucial for treating autoimmune disorders and managing organ transplants. Both segments address distinct health needs, reflecting the growing demand for targeted therapies that either enhance or regulate immune function in various medical conditions.</p></p>
