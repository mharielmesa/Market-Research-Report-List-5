<p><p>The nanoparticle-sizing equipment market is segmented into several types, including NanoSMPS, NanoCPC, and NanoElectrostatic Classifier, among others. NanoSMPS (Scanning Mobility Particle Sizer) measures particle size distribution by mobility in a gas, while NanoCPC (Condensation Particle Counter) counts particles based on their condensation growth. The NanoElectrostatic Classifier sorts particles by electrical mobility. Other equipment types may include dynamic light scattering devices and nanoparticle tracking analysis tools, each serving distinct applications in nanotechnology and material characterization.</p></p>
