<p><ul><li>With Adhesive Base</li><li>Without Adhesive Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB supports market is segmented into two main types: adhesive base and non-adhesive base supports. Adhesive base supports feature a sticky backing that allows for easy attachment to printed circuit boards (PCBs), providing stability during assembly and operation. In contrast, non-adhesive base supports rely on mechanical fixtures or clips to secure the PCB. Both types cater to different applications, with adhesive base supports often preferred for compact designs, while non-adhesive options offer easier removal and repositioning.</p></p>
