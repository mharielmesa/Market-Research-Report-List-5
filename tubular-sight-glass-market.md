<p><strong>The Tubular Sight Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flange Type</li><li>Thread Type</li><li>Welding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tubular sight glass market encompasses three primary types: flange type, thread type, and welding type. Flange type sight glasses are bolted between flanges, providing a secure and leak-proof connection, ideal for high-pressure applications. Thread type sight glasses utilize threaded connections for easy installation and maintenance, suitable for lower-pressure environments. Welding type sight glasses are welded directly to pipes, ensuring a robust and permanent fixture that can withstand extreme conditions. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on application requirements.</p></p>
