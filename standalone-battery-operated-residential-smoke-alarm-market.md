<p><p>Standalone battery-operated residential smoke alarms are essential for home safety, featuring different types based on detection technology. Photoelectric smoke alarms utilize a light beam to detect smoldering fires, making them effective for sensing slow-burning flames. Ionization smoke alarms use radioactive material to sense fast-flaming fires, offering quick detection in such scenarios. Combination smoke alarms merge both technologies, providing broad protection against various fire types. This diversity ensures homeowners can choose alarms that best fit their needs for fire safety.</p></p>
