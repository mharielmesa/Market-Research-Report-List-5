<p><strong>The Industrial & Marine Steam Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensing Steam Turbine</li><li>Non-Condensing Steam Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial & Marine Steam Turbine market is primarily categorized into two types: condensing and non-condensing steam turbines. Condensing steam turbines efficiently convert thermal energy into mechanical energy, using a condenser to extract heat from exhaust steam, maximizing efficiency and power output. In contrast, non-condensing steam turbines discharge steam directly into a process or heating system, prioritizing immediate steam use over efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in power generation and marine applications, depending on operational requirements and efficiency goals.</p></p>
