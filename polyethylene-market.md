<p><p>The polyethylene market is segmented primarily into three types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE), Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE), and Linear Low-Density Polyethylene (LLDPE). HDPE is known for its strength and durability, commonly used in containers and piping. LDPE is more flexible and is widely used in plastic bags and films. LLDPE combines properties of both and is favored for its tensile strength and elasticity, used in packaging and agricultural films. Other types include specialty polyethylenes catering to niche applications.</p></p>
