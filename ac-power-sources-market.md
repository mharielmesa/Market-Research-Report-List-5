<p><p>The AC power sources market is primarily divided into two types: single-phase and three-phase systems. Single-phase power is commonly used in residential applications, providing a simple, efficient way to power small appliances and lighting. In contrast, three-phase power is favored for industrial and commercial applications due to its ability to handle higher loads more efficiently. This system distributes power across three wires, resulting in smoother operation and reduced energy losses, making it ideal for heavy machinery and large installations.</p></p>
