<p><strong>The Equity Fund Sales Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Funds</li><li>Passive Funds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Equity fund sales occur within two main market types: active and passive funds. Active funds involve portfolio managers making strategic decisions to outperform a benchmark, actively buying and selling equities based on research and market analysis. Conversely, passive funds aim to replicate the performance of a specific index by holding all or a representative sample of its securities. This strategy typically results in lower fees, as there is less frequent trading and fewer management decisions involved.</p></p>
