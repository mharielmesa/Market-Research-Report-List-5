<p><p>The Thermal Ceramics market comprises two primary types: Ceramic Fibers and Insulation Bricks. Ceramic Fibers are lightweight, high-temperature materials used for insulation and refractory applications, offering excellent thermal stability and resistance. Insulation Bricks, on the other hand, are heavier and designed for thermal insulation in industrial furnaces and kilns, providing structural integrity and minimizing heat loss. Both types are essential for enhancing energy efficiency and performance in various high-temperature operations across industries such as steel, cement, and petrochemicals.</p></p>
