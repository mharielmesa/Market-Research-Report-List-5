<p><p>Industrial Electric Arc Furnaces (EAC) are key facilities in steelmaking and metal recycling, utilizing electric arcs to melt scrap or bulk materials. The AC Electric Arc Furnace (EAC) operates with alternating current, offering advantages like lower operational costs and flexibility for large-scale production. In contrast, DC Electric Arc Furnaces (EAC) use direct current, providing enhanced efficiency and better quality control due to more precise temperature management. Both types cater to different production needs and market demands within the metallurgical industry.</p></p>
