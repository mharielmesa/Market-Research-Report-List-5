<p><p>Vehicle disinfection systems are categorized into three main types: bracket type, tunnel type, and chassis type. Bracket type systems involve mounted disinfectant sprayers that can be positioned on existing structures or vehicles for targeted disinfection. Tunnel type systems create a passageway where vehicles are disinfected as they drive through, utilizing overhead and side sprays. Chassis type systems focus on disinfecting the undercarriage of vehicles, ensuring comprehensive cleaning, especially for heavy-duty vehicles. Each type caters to specific disinfecting needs in various environments.</p></p>
