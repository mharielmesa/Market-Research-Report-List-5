<p><p>The wound dressing pad market features two primary types: non-stick pads and sticky pads. Non-stick pads are designed to prevent the dressing from adhering to the wound, allowing for pain-free changes and reducing trauma during removal. They are often used for delicate or sensitive wounds. Conversely, sticky pads come with adhesive backing, ensuring secure placement over the wound and promoting stability. These pads provide a tight seal to protect against contaminants, making them suitable for various injuries and surgical sites.</p></p>
