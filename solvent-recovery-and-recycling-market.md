<p><p>The solvent recovery and recycling market encompasses two primary types: on-site and off-site solvent recycling. On-site solvent recycling involves the reuse of solvents within the same facility, allowing for immediate recovery, cost savings, and reduced environmental impact. Off-site solvent recycling, on the other hand, entails collecting solvents for processing at specialized facilities. This method provides a broader network for solvent recovery, catering to multiple businesses and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations while also enabling resource-efficient practices.</p></p>
