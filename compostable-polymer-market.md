<p><p>The compostable polymer market includes various types of biodegradable materials. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is a versatile bioplastic made from fermented plant starch, widely used in packaging. Polyhydroxy Alkanoates (PHA) are microbial-based polymers with excellent biodegradability. Polyesters like PBS, PBAT, and PCL combine durability with compostability, suitable for flexible applications. Cellulose derivatives, derived from plant fibers, provide eco-friendly alternatives for films and coatings. These materials cater to sustainable packaging, agriculture, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
