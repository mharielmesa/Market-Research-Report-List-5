<p><p>Passive fire protection coatings are essential for enhancing the fire resistance of structures. The market comprises mainly two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering benefits like low odor, easy cleanup, and environmental friendliness. They are ideal for interior applications. In contrast, solvent-based coatings use organic solvents, providing superior adhesion and durability, making them suitable for exterior applications. Both types aim to prevent fire spread and protect structural integrity during a fire incident.</p></p>
