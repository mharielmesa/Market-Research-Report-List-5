<p><p>The Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) slurry market consists of three primary types: alumina slurry, colloidal silica slurry, and ceria slurry. Alumina slurry is known for its effectiveness in polishing various substrates and providing good material removal rates. Colloidal silica slurry offers high planarization capability, especially for advanced microelectronic devices, due to its fine particle size. Ceria slurry is favored for its superior performance on certain materials, such as silicon wafers, providing enhanced surface finish and reduced defects in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
